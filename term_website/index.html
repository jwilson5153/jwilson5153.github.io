Bolivar Chamber of Commerce Purposes and Goals

Purposes

The Bolivar Area Chamber of Commerce, now in its 106th year of service, helps support and grow local business through education, networking, promotion and publicity; creating a positive culture for business growth for existing business as well as attracting new business. The chamber serves as a welcome center, tourism information center, and local information center for current, new, and potential businesses and residents, as well as visitors to our area. We are here to serve.

Goals

-Network with other businesses and community
-Gain a voice in the local business community
-Increase business contacts
-Share information about your business with potential customers
-Acquire customer referrals
-Expand your prospect base through chamber events
-Add credibility to your business
-Increase your visibility in the community
